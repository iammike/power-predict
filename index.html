Critical-power modeling from your Strava history

How many watts. For how long.

Power Predict fits a critical-power model to your best efforts and predicts the wattage you can sustain for a target race duration, weighted toward your recent fitness.

A coarse synthesis. CP is set to 0.95 × FTP (or used directly if you enter CP); W' comes from your 1-min sprint when given, otherwise defaults to 18 kJ (middle of the trained-cyclist range). Upload your archive when you can — the prediction tightens up considerably with real data.
